KINKIIZI HIGH SCHOOL-NYAKATARE

Kinkiizi high School is a church of Uganda founded,Government Aided “O” and “A” level, mixed, Day and Boarding Secondary School located in Kanungu town council, Kanungu District. The school boasts of having a well-stocked library, a fully equipped science laboratory with up to date laboratory equipment, a spacious main hall and a conducive green environment that enables students to read under the fresh natural air which helps them to concentrate. Khs is a home away from home for students.
